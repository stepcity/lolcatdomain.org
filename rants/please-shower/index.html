Please take a shower :(

... and other hygiene related tips by Dr. Seuss!

OK, in all seriousness, I need you guys to be better. This isn't just for the geeked out losers in my own community — EVERYBODY needs better hygiene.

Some rooms, deep down in the underbelly of my school, have a funk permeating within. I walk in and feel a wave of humid odor wash over me and I feel my fight-or-flight kick in. Someone sits down next to me and the odor gets worse... WHEN DOES IT END?!?!

And it's not just showering. That's just the most obvious one. But when I daily observe people walk away from a urinal, turn the sink on, turn it off, and leave (notice how they never stuck their hand in the water?), I feel genuine disappointment in the world.

Please guys. Wash your hands. Take a shower. Brush your teeth. Wear deodorant. I don't like how uncomfortable it feels to exist in public.
