Welcome!

There's not really much to say about me! I'd like to describe myself as a cool dude with some rad interests and based opinions. In the spirit of full disclosure, many of those are negative - I think there's a lot to dislike right now - but I try to keep a clear separation between that (my rants) and the things that are actually interesting (my posts).

So far, I haven't written much here, but I've got a lot of interests, including (but not limited to) (and very generally) computers, nature, and languages. Now just recursively add every sub- and super- interest, and you've got the complete list of my interests!
